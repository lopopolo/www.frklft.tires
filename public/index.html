Protect Your Fleet

FRKLFT is an enterprise, subscription-based forklift tire delivery platform.

Equipment failure is the leading cause for employee litigation in material handling and logistics environments. Manage your exposure to liability by regularly and automatically replacing the tires on your fleet of forklifts.

FRKLFT supplies tires for popular models of the leading manufactures, including Toyota, KION Group, Jungheinrich Group, and Crown Equipment.

FRKLFT's tires as a service package offers automated purchase orders, a variety of payment options, and 3 day delivery from one of our 26 warehouse locations in the USA.
